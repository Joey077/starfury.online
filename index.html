Kill the King

A strategic medieval adventure to overthrow the tyrant

Game Overview

Kill the King is an immersive strategic adventure set in a medieval kingdom ruled by a tyrannical monarch. As a rebel leader, your mission is to gather allies, resources, and plan the perfect strategy to overthrow the king.

Navigate through political intrigue, build your rebel army, and make crucial decisions that will determine the fate of the kingdom. Each playthrough offers unique challenges and multiple paths to victory.

Will you choose stealth and subterfuge, or raise an army for open rebellion? The choice is yours in this captivating browser-based strategy game.

Key Features

Strategic Gameplay

Plan your moves carefully, manage resources, and build alliances to increase your chances of success.

Multiple Endings

Your choices matter! Discover different story outcomes based on your decisions throughout the game.

Character Development

Interact with a cast of unique characters, each with their own motivations and storylines.

How to Play

  1. Begin by exploring the kingdom and gathering information about the king's weaknesses.
  2. Recruit allies from different factions to join your cause.
  3. Collect resources and weapons to strengthen your rebellion.
  4. Choose your approach: stealth, diplomacy, or direct confrontation.
  5. Execute your plan and adapt to unexpected events and consequences.
